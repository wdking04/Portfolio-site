WINSTON KING

Hi, thanks for visiting!


I’m Winston.  I recently graduated with a bachelor’s degree in software development and I’m pretty excited about it.  I’m a career-changer with a background of sales and sales management within the finance industry.  I have always been interested in technology and curious as to “what makes things work”.  As a kid I would take apart just about anything I could to see its inner workings then reassemble it.  Sometimes I would use the parts and try to create something new.  This seemingly never-ending curiosity led to my nickname “The Cat”.  Ok,  that last bit about the nickname I made up.  I just always wanted a cool nickname and never got one growing up.  Anyway,  this tinkering and problem solving as a child has lingered within me to adulthood and in some ways sparked my desire to pursue a career in tech.

Although just getting started on my developer’s journey,  my previous experiences have helped shape and develop me personally and professionally,  equipping me with traits and skills I believe can be applied to this new endeavor.  I’m a goal-setter with an ambition for knowledge and growth.  I am looking forward to expanding my proficiency and creating new things.
